Chicken Tikka Masala

Ingredients

  • 1 cup rice
  • 1 1/2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 1/2 pounds boneless, skinless chicken thighs, cut into chunks
  • salt and pepper
  • 1/2 medium sweet onion, diced
  • 1 tablespoon minced garlic
  • 1 tablespoon minced ginger
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons garam masala
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons chili powder
  • 1 1/2 teaspoons ground turmeric
  • 15-ounce tomato sauce
  • 1 cup chicken stock
  • 1/2 cup heavy cream
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro leaves

Steps

  1. Cook rice
  2. Heat canola oil on pan
  3. Season chicken with salt and pepper
  4. Add chicken and onion to pan
  5. Cook until ready
  6. Pour tomato sauce and chicken stock to pot and heat
  7. Add garlic, ginger, garam masala, chili powder, and turmeric
  8. Heat desired thickness
  9. Add heavy cream until desired taste
