Slow-Roasted Salmon with Fennel, Citrus, and Chiles

Ingredients

  • 1 fennel bulb
  • 1 blood/navel orange; sliced
  • 1 lemon; sliced
  • 1 red Fresno chile or jalapeno
  • 1 dill
  • salt and pepper
  • 1 2-pound salmon fillet; center-cut
  • olive oil

Steps

  1. Preheat oven to 275
  2. Toss fennel, orange slices, lemon slices, chile, & dill
  3. Season salmon with salt & pepper
  4. pour oil
  5. oven cooking time: 30-40 min
  6. Inner Temp: 145 F
